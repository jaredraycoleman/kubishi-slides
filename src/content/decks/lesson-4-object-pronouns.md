# Object Pronoun Prefixes

The object goes on the verb as a prefix.

| Subject | Object prefix | Meaning |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| `nüü` | `i-` | I, me |
| `üü` | `ü-` | you |
| `taa` | `ta-` | you & I |
| `uhu` | `u-` | he/she/it (far) |
| `mahu` | `a-`, `ma-` | he/she/it (near) |

---

# Object Pronoun Prefixes — Plurals

| Subject | Object prefix | Meaning |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| `nüügwa` | `ni-` | we (me & others) |
| `üügwa` | `üi-` | you all |
| `taagwa` | `tei-` | we (you & I & others) |
| `uhuŵa` | `ui-` | they (far) |
| `mahuŵa` | `ai-`, `mai-` | they (near) |

# Vocab

- `Isha'` — coyote
- `Pahabichi` — bear
- `Kamü` — jackrabbit
- `Tühidda` — deer
- `Tüka-` — to eat
- `Hibi-` — to drink
- `Puhei-` — to search for
- `Puni-` — to see

---

Isha'-uu hibi-ti. | The coyote is drinking.
Isha'-uu a-hibi-ti. | The coyote is drinking it.
Pahabichi-ii puni-ti. | The bear is seeing.
Pahabichi-ii a-buni-ti. | The bear is seeing it.
Kamü-ii tüka-ti. | The jackrabbit is eating.
Kamü-ii i-düka-ti. | The jackrabbit is eating me!
Tühidda-ii puhei-ti. | The deer is searching.
Tühidda-ii tei-buhei-ti. | The deer is searching for us (you & I & others).

---

Pahabichi-uu a-hibi-ti. | The bear is drinking it.
Isha'-uu i-düka-ti. | The coyote is eating me.
Kamü-ii ui-buni-ti. | The jackrabbit is seeing them.
Pahabichi-ii ni-buhei-ti. | The bear is searching for us (me & others).
Isha'-uu ma-buni-ti. | The coyote is seeing it (nearby).
Kamü-ii ai-hibi-ti. | The jackrabbit is drinking them (nearby).
Tühidda-uu u-buhei-ti. | The deer is searching for it (far).
Pahabichi-ii tei-buhei-ti. | The bear is searching for you & I.

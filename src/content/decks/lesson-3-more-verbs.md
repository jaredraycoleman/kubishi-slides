# More Verbs

| Verb | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| `Yotsi` | to fly |
| `Wünü` | to stand |
| `Üwi` | to sleep |
| `Nüga` | to dance |

These work just like the verbs in Lesson 1 — add `-ti` for present tense, then add a subject (pronoun from L1, or noun from L2).

---

Üwi-ti isha'-uu. | The coyote is sleeping.
Üügwa mia-ti. | You (and others) are going.
Pahabichi-uu poyoha-ti. | The bear is running.
Kamü-ii katü-ti. | The jackrabbit is sitting.
Tüka-ti mahu. | He/She is eating.
Yotsi-ti kwi'naa-uu. | The eagle is flying.
Wünü-ti pungizhi-uu. | The mouse is standing.
Kooipa-uu nüga-ti. | The Bighorn sheep is dancing.

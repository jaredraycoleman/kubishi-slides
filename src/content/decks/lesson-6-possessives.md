# Possessive Prefixes

| Prefix | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `i-` | my |
| `ta-` | your & my (inclusive) |
| `ü-` | your |
| `a-` / `ma-` | his/her (nearby, visible) |
| `u-` | his/her (far, not present) |
| `tü-` | his/her own |

---

Isha'-uu hibi-ti. | The coyote is drinking.
Isha'-uu tü-paya-nieka a-hibi-ti. | The coyote is drinking his own water.
Isha'-uu u-paya-nieka a-hibi-ti. | The coyote is drinking his/her (someone else's) water.
Pahabichi-ii tüba-neika a-düka-ti. | The bear is eating the pine nuts.
Pahabichi-uu i-düba-neika a-düka-ti! | The bear is eating my pine nuts!
I-isha'bugu-uu tüka-ti. | My dog is eating.
Ü-bagwi-uu i-bagwi-noka u-düka-ti! | Your fish is eating my fish!
Ma-dabuutsi'-uu poyoha-ti. | His/her (nearby) cottontail is running.
Nüü u-kaw̃a-noka u-buni-ti. | I see his/her (far) wood rat.
